Шуба – это деликатная вещь, которая требует деликатного ухода. Как и любая вещь, шуба изнашивается и теряет свой первозданный блеск и шелковистость. Причиной тому – многочисленные атмосферные осадки, загрязнения областей непосредственного контакта с кожей, таких как: воротник, рукава и другие виды загрязнений. Бережный уход BIOFUR собирает в себе всю силу природных компонентов, предлагая инновационное решение в чистке и уходе за меховыми изделиями

BIOFUR

CLEANER

BIOFUR Cleaner is used for thorough fur cleaning and removal of all kinds of easily removable contaminations. Due to a special combination of natural components BIOFUR Cleaner quickly and effectively removes easily removable contaminations without damaging your fur garment

HOW TO USE

1. Take the oilcloth from your BIOFUR set.

2. Unfold the oilcloth and spread it on a flat firm surface of a table.

3. Lay your fur garment on the oilcloth and carefully examine your fur garment (fig. 1). If you find general contaminations on your fur garment, use Cleaner from the set to remove them.

4. Take the Cleaner bottle in your hands and shake it well.

5. Remove the cap from the bottle.

6. Evenly spray the cleaner in a thin coat on the whole surface of your garment from a distance not less than 15 sm (fig. З). ATTENTION: Avoid soaking of your garment.

7. In places with more visible and severe contamination make 2-3 sprays (one spray - one pressing of the spray button).

8. Roll your garment with the fur inside and leave it for 15-20 minutes.

9. After 15-20 minutes unroll your garment and remove remaining contaminations and the sprayed Cleaner using the terry-cloth napkin from the set. Periodically and carefully rinse the napkin under a jet of warm water and wring it out (fig. 5).

10. Put your garment treated as it was described above on a coat hanger and let it dry completely in a dry well-aired place at room temperature, far from heating household appliances and direct sun light (fig. 6). Periodically comb the fur during drying in pile direction using the fur brush from the set (fig. 7).

ATTENTION: Make sure you placed your garment on a coat hanger symmetrically to keep its geometry and avoid its deformation. Repeat the procedure if necessary, but first wait until your garment is completely dry.

ATTENTION: after you apply the Cleaner, it is necessary to use BIOFUR Restorer having studied the application guide.

BIOFUR

Restorer

BIOFUR restorer is used to restore of natural fur structure and to prevent further contamination and for final treatment after application of BIOFUR Cleaner. Specially developed formula of BIOFUR Restorer due to a particular combination of natural components freshens up your garment and restores natural fur shine.

HOW TO USE

1. Take the oilcloth from your BIOFUR set.

2. Unfold the oilcloth and spread it on a flat firm surface of a table.

3. Lay your fur garment on the oilcloth and carefully examine your fur garment (fig. 1).

4. Take the Restorer bottle in your hands and shake it well.

5. Turn the holder under the spray handle in OPEN position.

6. Evenly spray BIOFUR Restorer on the whole surface of your dry fur garment from a distance not less than 15 sm (fig. З).

7. Then remove the sprayed solution using the terry-cloth napkin from the set. Periodically and carefully rinse the napkin under a jet of warm water and wring it out (fig. 5). ATTENTION: Avoid soaking of your garment.

8.Put your garment treated as it was described above on a coat hanger and let it dry completely in a dry well-aired place at room temperature, far from heating household appliances and direct sun light (fig. 6). Periodically comb the fur during drying in pile direction using the fur brush from the set (fig. 7). ATTENTION: Make sure you placed your garment on a coat hanger symmetrically to keep its geometry and avoid its deformation..

ATTENTION: if your fur garment was exposed to rain and snow or was kept for a long time in a garment bag or the fur on your fur garment was flattened (fig. 9), got dull or simply lost its original appearance, you can try to restore your fur garment using BIOFUR Restorer. In this case you will need to do all steps described above without using our BIOFUR Cleaner and/or BIOFUR Soap.

BIOFUR

SOAP

BIOFUR soap is used for through removal of all kinds of local contaminations from your fur garment.

HOW TO USE

1. Take the oilcloth from your BIOFUR set.

2. Unfold the oilcloth and spread it on a flat firm surface of a table.

3. Lay your fur garment on the oilcloth and carefully examine your fur garment (fig. 1).

4. Take the soap bottle in your hands and shake it well.

5. Remove the cap from the bottle and spray some soap on the tip of the brush included in the set (fig. 2). One or two sprays of the soap on the brush are enough.

6. Clean out places with local contaminations using the brush from the set with some sprayed soap on it (fig. 2). ATTENTION: Do not rub your fur garment with the brush too much.

7. Remove the existing local contamination and excess of sprayed soap using a terry-cloth napkin from the set. Regularly and carefully rinse the terry-cloth napkin under a jet of warm water and wring it out (fig. 5). ATTENTION: Avoid soaking of your garment..

8. Put your garment treated as described above on a coat hanger and let it dry in a dry well-aired place at room temperature, far from household heating appliances and direct sun light (fig. 6). Periodically comb the fur in the pile direction with a fur brush with a metal bristle (fig. 7).

Drying your fur garment, place in a way to keep its geometry and to avoid its deformation. Repeat the procedure if necessary, but first wait until your garment is completely dry.
ATTENTION: after you apply the Soap, it is necessary to use the BIOFUR Cleaner having studied the application guide.
ATTENTION: BIOFUR soap does not guarantee complete removal of very old stains and persistent stains (such as blood, champagne, red wine, oil products and similar stains).
